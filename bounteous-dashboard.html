Quick Qualification Test — Ask These 5 Questions
1. What is your current GCC headcount in India and annual all-in cost?
If <1000 people and cost >$10M — it's a GCC Takeout candidate. Proceed immediately.
2. How many AI pilots have you run in the last 18 months — and how many are in production?
If pilots ≥ 3 and production = 0, they are stuck. This is our most winnable deal type.
3. What is your attrition rate in the GCC and your average tenure of senior engineers?
Attrition >18% is a red flag for the client and a green flag for Bounteous.
4. Do you have a board-level mandate to reduce operational cost or improve engineering velocity?
A board mandate = urgency = faster deal cycle. Always qualify for executive sponsor.
5. What is your current vendor landscape for engineering — SI, boutique, or in-house?
Heavy SI dependency = we lead with agility + domain depth. In-house GCC = lead with cost + AI value.

How to Impress in the Room
🎯

Open with Their Pain, Not Our Story

Lead with a data point specific to their sector — e.g., "GCCs in your peer group are spending 28% more per FTE than 2 years ago. Here's why." Show we've done the homework before we walked in.

⚡

Live AI Demo — Not a Slide

In every executive meeting, show a working AI agent relevant to their business — KYC extraction, document reconciliation, or a code review bot. Real outputs. Real seconds. Nothing accelerates trust faster.

📊

Pre-Built ROI for Their Numbers

Walk in with a rough business case using their public headcount data and market cost benchmarks. Show what a 30% AI-optimised GCC saves them specifically. Make it personal.

🏆

Reference at Peer Level

Name a client in their industry (with permission) and offer a peer reference call. Executives trust peers far more than vendors. One reference call is worth five slide decks.

🔓

Go At-Risk on 60-Day POC

Offer to run a fully funded or at-risk 60-day AI POC. If we don't deliver the metric, they don't pay or we refund. This converts sceptics into champions overnight.

🤝

Co-Create, Don't Present

Run the AI Co-Innovation Studio as a working session, not a pitch. When clients co-create the solution, they own it — and they champion it internally. It is the most powerful sales tool we have.
