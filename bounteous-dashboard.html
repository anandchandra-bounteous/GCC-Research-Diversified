Quick Qualification — 5 Questions
1. What is your GCC headcount and annual all-in cost?
If <1000 people and cost >$10M — it's a GCC Playbook candidate. Qualify which theme fits: Takeout, Build & Scale, or Location Transition.
2. How many AI pilots in the last 18 months are now in production?
If pilots ≥ 3 and production = 0, they are stuck. This is our most winnable deal type.
3. What is your GCC attrition rate and average senior engineer tenure?
Attrition >18% is a red flag for the client and a green flag for Bounteous.
4. Is there a board mandate to reduce cost or improve engineering velocity?
A board mandate = urgency = faster deal cycle. Always qualify for executive sponsor access.
5. What is your current vendor landscape — SI, boutique, or in-house GCC?
Heavy SI dependency = lead with agility + domain depth. In-house GCC = lead with cost + AI value.

How to Impress in the Room
🎯

Open with Their Pain

Lead with a data point specific to their sector — "GCCs in your peer group are spending 28% more per FTE than 2 years ago." Show you've done the homework before walking in.

⚡

Live AI Demo — Not a Slide

Show a working AI agent in every exec meeting — KYC extraction, document reconciliation, or a code review bot. Real outputs in real seconds. Nothing accelerates trust faster.

📊

Pre-Built ROI for Their Numbers

Walk in with a rough business case using their public headcount data. Show what a 30% AI-optimised GCC saves them specifically. Make it personal.

🏆

Reference at Peer Level

Name a client in their industry and offer a peer reference call. One reference call is worth five slide decks — executives trust peers, not vendors.

🔓

Go At-Risk on 60-Day POC

Offer a fully funded or at-risk 60-day AI POC. If we don't deliver the metric, they don't pay. This converts sceptics into champions overnight.

🤝

Co-Create, Don't Present

Run the AI Co-Innovation Studio as a working session. When clients co-create the solution they own it — and champion it internally. Our most powerful sales tool.
